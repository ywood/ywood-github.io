About the art

Creating the landscapes

The landscapes are created through a process inspired by the wool trade. The natural wool is taken and through "teasing", "carding" and naturally dyeing the artwork you see is created.

Inspiration

After moving to the Cotswolds, I was inspired by the deep history this area shares with the textile industry.

Commissions

I am also able to undertake commissions using your photographs with consultation, to capture memories of places and landscapes important to you.

History and education

I gained an Honours Degree in Fine Art from Central St Martin's School of Art, and have regularly exhibited sculpture in London and other parts of the UK.
